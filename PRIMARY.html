PRE-SCHOOL AND GRADE SCHOOL ADMISSION

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Junior Kinder - 4 years old
Senior Kinder - 5 years old

GRADE ONE

1. Six (6) years old by January of the incoming School Year. 2. One (1) year in Pre-School
3. Can read and write
4. Satisfactorily passed the Entrance Test and Interview
5. Form 138

OLD PUPILS

Report Card and Reservation Slip

TRANSFEREES

Grade 2-6 (See Requirement Nos. 3, 4 and 5)
1. Can read and write
2. Satisfactorily passed the Entrance Test and Interview
3. Form 138

SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AIDS

Discounts Based On Academic Performance

• 1st Honors - 100% discount on tuition fees
• 2nd Honors - 50% discount on tuition fees
• 3rd Honors - 30% discount on tuition fees
• Most Outstanding Junior Kinder - 30% discount on tuition fees

Note: Transferees and New Students are entitled to the scholarship after passing qualifying exams.

Family Discounts

A discount on tuition fees may be applied for three or more children from a single family who are concurrently enrolled in the college, regardless of years of residence. Discounts on tuition fees are applicable to the youngest members of the family.
• 3rd and 4th Child - 15% discount on tuition fees
• 4th and 5th Child - 20% discount on tuition fees
• 5th and 6th Child - 25% discount on tuition fees
• 6th and 7th Child - 30% discount on tuition fees
