SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL ADMISSION

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

1. Apply at the Guidance Office.
2. Pay a minimal entrance examination fee at the Accounting Office.
3. Get your entrance examination schedule, followed by a brief interview after the exam.
4. Results will be available one week after the examination.
5. Prepare the following for the enrollment:
o Certificate of Good Moral Character
o Birth Certificate (Xerox)
o Report Card
o Original Transcript of records (F-137)

SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AIDS

Jose G. Olivares Scholarship Grant

A full scholarship amounting to a full waiver in tuition fees is granted to students who are awarded First Honors (Valedictorian).

Leonora J. Olivares Scholarship Grant

A partial scholarship with a 50% discount in tuition fees is granted to students who are awarded Second Honors (Salutatorian).

Maria Juzgaya Scholarship Grant

A partial scholarship with a 30% discount in tuition fees is granted to students who are awarded Third Honors (First Honorable Mention).

St. Aloysius Gonzaga Scholarship Grant

• Service Grant - A scholarship grant given to non-academic recipients with full/partial discount in tuition fee.
• Financial Grant - A scholarship grant given to academic recipients with full/partial discount in tuition fee.

The Fund for Assistance to Private Education

It is an organization which to promote the development of private education in the Philippines. It pursues this mission by helping private schools in their quest for academic excellence, managerial competence and financial viability, through the extension of logistic and technical support.
• Educational Voucher System (EVS)
• Education Service Contracting (ESC)

Family Discounts

A discount on tuition fees may be applied for three or more children from a single family who are concurrently enrolled in the college, regardless of years of residence. Discounts on tuition fees are applicable to the youngest members of the family
• 3rd and 4th Child - 15% discount on tuition fees
• 4th and 5th Child - 20% discount on tuition fees
