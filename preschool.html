PRE-SCHOOL AND GRADE SCHOOL

"DEVELOP THE FULL POTENTIAL OF GROWING CHILDREN"

The Curriculum of the Pre-School and Grade School of St. Louis College Valenzuela is optimized to develop the full potential of growing children. Hence, the school's programs are child-centered, cognizant of multiple intelligence and based on the total Christian Formation balanced by academic, extra-curricular and enrichment activities.


Pre-School Curriculum:

- Christian Living and Character Formation
- Language Development
- Reading Readines
- Math Conceptualization
- Science and Health
- Filipino
- Music and Rhythm
- Computer Aided Instruction
- Practical Life Program
- Visual and Performing Arts

Grade School Curriculum:

- Christian Living and Character Formation
- Language and Reading Profiency
- Mathematics
- Science and Health
- Filipino
- Civics, Culture and Geography
- Music, Arts and Sports
- Home Economics and Livelihood Education
- Computer literacy
- Visual and Performing Arts
