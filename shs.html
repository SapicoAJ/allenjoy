SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

"IMPROVE THE LISTENING SKILLS IN THE ENGLISH LANGUAGE"

The Senior High School in particular offers college special partnerships and bridging subjects that includes the Test of English for International Communication (TOEIC) program to help students improve their listening skills in the English language to help further their education or career - as well a Road Safety, Personal Finance and Relationship Managemen

These programs are geared towards the further development of the inherent talents and skills of the students. The school seeks to mold their character based on Christian values, to prepare them for higher education, to realize the potentials of the student to become productive and responsible members of Filipino society.
